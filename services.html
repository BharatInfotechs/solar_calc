Residential Solar Installation

Complete rooftop solar systems for homes. We handle site survey, design, net-metering paperwork, installation, and maintenance. Reduce your electricity bills by up to 90% with government subsidies under PM Surya Ghar Yojana.

  • 25-year panel warranty
  • Free site inspection

Commercial Solar Solutions

Scalable solar plants for offices, schools, hospitals, and factories. CAPEX and OPEX models available. Achieve ROI in 3-4 years while cutting operational costs and carbon footprint significantly.

  • 10kW to 1MW+ capacity
  • Remote monitoring system
  • GST & tax benefit assistance

Solar Maintenance & AMC

Keep your solar plant running at peak efficiency. Our annual maintenance contracts include panel cleaning, inverter checks, performance monitoring, and on-call support within 24 hours in Lucknow region.

  • Quarterly cleaning service
  • Performance reports
  • Emergency repair support

Solar Battery & Hybrid Systems

Power backup solutions with lithium battery storage. Perfect for areas with frequent outages. Store excess solar energy during day and use it at night. Hybrid inverters with grid + solar + battery.

  • 5kWh to 50kWh storage
  • Smart energy management
  • 10-year battery warranty

All-in-one integrated solar street lights for societies, highways, and rural areas. Auto dusk-to-dawn operation, motion sensors available. Zero electricity bills and minimal maintenance required.
