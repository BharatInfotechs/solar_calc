What We Provide

Expert solar consultation and end-to-end project support — we don't sell panels, we deliver solutions

Our Core Offerings

We are solar consultants and project facilitators. Our role is to guide you through every step of your solar journey — from feasibility to commissioning. We don't manufacture or deal in products; we provide expertise, planning, and execution support.

📊

Solar Feasibility Study

We provide detailed site analysis and energy audits to determine your solar potential. Our reports cover shadow analysis, load assessment, roof strength evaluation, and ROI projections. This helps you make an informed decision before investing a single rupee.

Analysis & Planning
📐

System Design & Engineering

We provide custom solar system designs optimized for your specific energy needs and site conditions. Our engineering team creates single-line diagrams, structural layouts, and BOS selection. We ensure maximum generation with minimum losses, compliant with all DISCOM standards.

Technical Design
📝

Liaison & Documentation Support

We provide complete assistance with government approvals, net-metering applications, and subsidy paperwork under PM Surya Ghar Yojana. Our team handles UPNEDA submissions, DISCOM coordination, and vendor selection so you don't get stuck in red tape.

Regulatory Support
👷

Project Management & Supervision

We provide on-ground project supervision to ensure quality installation by your chosen EPC vendor. Our engineers monitor work progress, conduct quality checks, and verify commissioning. We act as your technical representative on-site.

Execution Oversight
📈

Performance Monitoring Setup

We provide remote monitoring system configuration and training. Track your solar generation, savings, and system health through mobile apps and dashboards. We set up alerts for faults and provide monthly performance reports to ensure optimal output.

Operations
🎓

Training & Handover

We provide comprehensive training to your team on system operation, basic troubleshooting, and safety protocols. Post-commissioning, we hand over all documentation, warranties, and provide 90-day support to ensure smooth transition.

Knowledge Transfer

Why Work With Us?

We are vendor-neutral consultants. Since we don't sell or manufacture solar products, our recommendations are unbiased and focused only on what's best for your project. We have facilitated 200+ solar installations across Uttar Pradesh without being tied to any single brand.

Our team includes certified solar engineers, former DISCOM officials, and project managers with 8+ years in the renewable energy sector. We speak your language — not sales jargon.
